OLE to Jakarta

I embarked on a 5 day overseas learning experience to Jakarta as part of a student exchange program. There I had the chance to experience what it was like to be a student in Jakarta, and also the diverse cultures found there.

I also had a buddy who had arrived to Singapore a few weeks prior as part of the exchange program. We spent time with each other and learnt much about how different our lives are despite our close geographical proximities.

School visit @ Penabur Secondary Tanjung Duren

Conveniently, we arrived at around the time of Indonesia's National Day, meaning I participated in their celebrations, where I learnt about the diverse cultures of the different Indonesian Islands, and also took part in some local games.

Furthermore, I took part in some of their normal lessons where I experienced what it was like learning in their school. We were there for 3 days to fully immerse ourselves in the experience.

Site visits

A tour guide brought us to many sites in Jakarta such as Taman Mini Indonesia, and the National Monument. We were taught about the rich history of many of these sites.
